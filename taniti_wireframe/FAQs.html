Frequently Asked


When is the best time to visit Taniti?

The rainy season begins in June and typically lasts through September. Taniti also enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.



What currency is used in Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.



What voltage output are the power outlets in Taniti?

The standard power outlet on the island is a 120 volt United States type outlet.



What are the liquor laws in Taniti?

It is illegal for alcohol to be served and sold between 12:00 a.m. and 9:00 a.m. The drinking age on Taniti is 18 years of age.



I don't speak Tanitian, how difficult will it be to get directions?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English and are happy to assist you! Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents. Maps and guidebooks can be purchased in many languages.
